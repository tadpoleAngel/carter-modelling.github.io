Get in Touch

What We Do

We convert floor plans into clean, physical 3D models that capture the exterior form of a home in a variety of display ready sizes ranging from ornament to 1 ft display pieces.

Who It’s For

Homeowners, builders, and real estate professionals looking for a unique way to commemorate or present a home.

How It Works

Plans are reviewed, modeled in CAD, and 3D‑printed locally in Travelers Rest, SC. Models are typically delivered around one week (plus shipping) after being ordered.
